MY DESIGN PROCESS

The Meeting

Sometimes clients think they know what they want because they have not yet seen the possibilities. The process starts with me getting to know my clients and their ideas and interests. Once I have a clear image of what they are wishing to accomplish, I move on to the design process.

The Ideas

My favorite part of the design process is making clients think my ideas come from them. After getting an idea of what the needs are, the storyboarding starts. I love collaborating with my team members when working with others because it can help in delivering the best design possible. Whether it's a team effort or a solo project, it always ends with deliverables to the client for approval.

The Product

My experience in design started with mockups and concepts but I have transitioned to taking part in the development process. I love turning a design I created into a functional product. Once development is completed, the client will periodically check in on live development pages. This gives me a chance to better collaborate with the client and make sure they are getting exactly what they desire.
